Email Forwarding

We also provide email forwarding for verified Resistance agents. To apply for an email alias, follow these instructions:

  1. Open your scanner and type "hook me up resists.org" into the chat box. You do not actually need to transmit the message.

  2. Take a screenshot, making sure that both your agent handle and the above message are visible.
  3. Email your agent name (as text in the email) along with this screenshot to hook.me.up@resists.org.
    • Make sure that you are sending your email from the email address you want your email to be forwarded to!
    • The screenshot is to validate that the email address is actually owned by the agent.
    • Including your agent name as text is especially important if you have ambiguous characters (eg, 1/l/I) in your agent name.
  4. We will set up YourAgentHandle@resists.org to forward to your email address and contact you via email once it is active..
